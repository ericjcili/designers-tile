About Us

We are a family-owned and operated business located in Coral Gables, Florida.

Serving the tile industry for three decades, our experience and dedication truly set us apart. As a distributor we have an opportunity to deliver a product to satisfy any imagination. Some of our products include: Glass, ceramic, porcelain, and various stones from around the world.
